Hi I'm Kayla and I'm a graphic designer and content creator from Philly. I'm a third year student at Thomas Jefferson University studying Graphic Design with a minor in Marketing. I have been drawing and painting since she was 5 years old.

I illustrated the Youth Poet Laureate’s poetry book “Daydreaming” and logo design for the podcast, “Black is Infinite”. I'm inspired by Monet, Emory Douglas and other impressionist artists. Also, I am an advocate for local government and representation in graphic design. My goal is to make an impact on my community through art, whether that be creating murals or building library boxes on a local trail.
